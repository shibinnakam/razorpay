Public Policy & Privacy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

Privacy Policy

At Faith Community Church, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard the data you provide to us.

  • Information Collection: We collect information such as name, email address, and phone number when you voluntarily provide it through our contact forms or donation portal.
  • Use of Information: We use your information to communicate with you, process donations, and provide you with updates about church activities. We never sell or rent your data to third parties.
  • Data Security: We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. Payment transactions are processed through secure, encrypted gateways.

Donation & Refund Policy

Financial contributions to Faith Community Church are used to support our mission and ministry programs. As a general rule, all donations are non-refundable.

However, if you feel an error has been made in your donation processing (such as a duplicate charge), please contact our administrative office within 30 days. We will work with you to resolve the issue as quickly as possible.

Terms of Use

By using this website, you agree to comply with and be bound by the following terms and conditions of use. The content of this website is for your general information and use only. It is subject to change without notice.

Unauthorized use of this website may give rise to a claim for damages and/or be a criminal offense.

If you have any questions about our policies, please contact us.
