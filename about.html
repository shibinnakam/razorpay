Our Story

Founded on Faith, Growing in Love

Faith Community Church was founded in 1995 with a handful of families who shared a simple vision: to create a place where anyone could find hope, healing, and a sense of belonging.

Over the last three decades, our church has grown from a living room gathering to a diverse community of believers who are passionate about sharing God's love through practical service and meaningful worship.

Our Core Values

These principles guide everything we do as a community.

Worship

We believe that worship is more than just Sunday songs—it's a lifestyle of gratitude and devotion to God.

Service

A faith that doesn't serve isn't complete. We are committed to rolling up our sleeves and helping those in need.

Community

No one is meant to walk through life alone. We foster relationships where we can support and encourage one each other.
